The Wiki Loves Monuments competition is one of the largest open photography contests in the world — hundreds of thousands of entries annually from six continents.

Winning at this level — especially internationally — signals genuine photographic mastery: composition, technical quality, cultural sensitivity, and the ability to find meaning in built heritage.

About the Competition
Supported by the Wikimedia Foundation and UNESCO, Wiki Loves Monuments runs across 50+ countries. In peak years it attracted over 350,000 photographs from 15,000+ participants — making any national or global win exceptional.
